Logos

Emblem logo for IT/Architecture school

The client wishes the school to succeed and be loved for the next century. Therefore I chose this traditional classic emblem style. The school mainly teaches IT and architecture, so I created a laptop, compass and ruler motif.

Company logo

The company is engaged in solar power generation and real estate business. The company’s vision is a good society, and live with renewable energy. I create a logo that I imagined we are protecting the earth and generating good energy. I chose the colour orange as solar, green as renewable and blue as clean earth colour.

Clinic logo

The client wished to use the word “St.” and a heartbeat motif somewhere in the logo. The colour I choose reflects a clean new clinic.
