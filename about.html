I am Mayumi, and I create web sites. I am interested in Web design and desital design.
I was born and raised in Japan. I lived in London UK for six years as a web editor. Now I am based in Ireland Limerick, I create websites for own projects while working full time job as a search quality analyst.

I studies Web design at Institute of Technology Carlow. Through this course I gained experience and technical skills in design, programming, prototype and web development.
When I lived in London, I worked as a Content Editor at Nichiei Limited (ChelseaGardens UK: Japanese E-commerce fashion company) for six years. I gained experience using with Photoshop, Illustrator and Dreamweaver to edit websites.
I have been participating in different design competitions for logo and book covers to gain experience with the hope of working in the desital design industry. By trying to apply a lot of different themes to up-skill my design techniques. I like keeping things simple and minimalistic. I love creating and designing to make people smile. I am a curious, dedicated and creative who loves design to experiment and learn new things.
